The 2nd Annual Reclaim Your Domain Hackathon

July 19th and 20th, 2014 Pepperdine West LA Campus

A group of committed educators and programmers will be convening in Los Angeles for a hackathon to extend and develop the ideas around "Reclaim Your Domain". http://reclaimyourdomain.org/ Specific focus will be on hacking an infrastructure for syndicating, securing and managing third-part service data, as well as working together to clearly articulate the rationale and vision for this work.

These efforts build on an array of ongoing initiatives, as well as last year's hackathon at MIT: http://open.media.mit.edu/hackathon/

The event will bring together people who have made impressive progress in these areas, though there is still a great deal of thinking, sharing, and creating to be done.
